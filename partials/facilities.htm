Horse Camping

We are the only RV park/campground that accepts horses and mules. Bring your own electric fence, panels, tethering, etc. We provide a wheel barrel & a shovel.

We are close to all of the trails in the Lincoln National Forest as well as the trails at Fort Stanton managed by the BLM. You will need to book your reservation soon to guarantee your accommodation.

Drive a little and save some money. We are just minutes North of Ruidoso. We are close to the horse race track in Ruidoso Downs, the casinos, dining, fishing, hunting, shopping and all other area attractions and events.

Our resort is located in the mountains and woods North of Ruidoso in a quiet, peaceful and serene forest setting in a small valley with wonderful scenic views and beautiful sunrises and sunsets.
